## How to Demo This Project (Suggested Walkthrough)

1. **Home / Recommend page** — enter sample values (e.g. N=90, P=42, K=43, Temp=20.9, Humidity=82, pH=6.5, Rainfall=202.9) and show the Rice recommendation, confidence chart, soil health score, and fertilizer advice
2. **Download PDF Report** — click the download button to show the generated PDF
3. **Compare Crops** — compare Rice vs Maize under the same conditions, show the radar chart and winner
4. **Dashboard** — walk through the NPK-by-crop chart, correlation heatmap, and rainfall vs temperature scatter plot
5. **Research Insights** — explain the K-Means clustering and how it supports Scenario 3 (research/policy planning)
6. **Live Weather Autofill** — click "Use My Location's Weather" to show real-time data being pulled in
